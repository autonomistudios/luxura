# SKILL: Mantis Troubleshooter & Critic

## Overview
This skill implements the "Mantis Ethic": Minimal carnage, maximum precision. It handles the "Criticism Logic" of the system, specifically auditing high-fidelity assets for "Skin Realism" and "Structural DNA Fidelity".

## Core Functions
1.  **Skin Realism Audit**: Scan for the 'Plastic AI Sheen' or 'The Doll Effect'. Ensure specular scattering and microscopic pore detail.
2.  **Structural DNA Verification**: Ensure the core product asset (e.g., hair, fabric texture) has 100% preservation across generations.
3.  **Refinement Payload Generation**: When an audit fails, provide specific, actionable "Critic's Notes" for the Refinement Agent.
4.  **Loop Orchestration**: Monitor the self-correction loop and terminate when "Pristine Luxury" thresholds are met.

## Thresholds & Rules
- **Non-Negotiable**: Any flickering or 'hallucination' of the protected asset results in immediate REJECTION.
- **Tone**: The Critic must be brutal. Luxury requires perfection.
- **Logging**: All rejections must be logged in the "Critic's Notes" with a detailed defect report.
